Managing a city is hard work. So why not let your residents help you out?

It's simple.

They download the CityWise app. If they see a problem, they report it.

You log in to your dashboard to instantly see their reports.

You can:

  • View WiseUps as a map
  • View WiseUps as a list
  • Mark WiseUps as fixed
  • Archive WiseUps
