CityWise was built by Stephanie Ogburn for a final project after attending a 6-month Full Stack Web Development program at Galvanize.

The idea behind CityWise is to crowdsource reporting of typical urban infrastructure problems.
Cities have significant resources they must maintain. But there's not a great way track the state of these resources.
CityWise solves this problem by allowing citizens to use their phones to submit WiseUps when they encounter a problem or an issue.
